  1. Walla Walla, WA

July 14, 2015


I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today: "Passing cars, When you can't see, May get you, A glimpse, Of eternity." I definitely won't be passing any cars.


June 2, 2015

My first day of the trip! I can't believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I'm on a Segway, I wasn't able to bring a whole lot with me:

  • cellphone
  • iPod
  • digital camera
  • a protein bar
Just the essentials.


As Lao Tzu would have said,

"A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway."
